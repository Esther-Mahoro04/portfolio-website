About Me

I am a first-year Software Engineering student at African Leadership University (ALU), driven by a passion for technology and its potential to transform healthcare. My goal is to bridge the gap between innovative technology solutions and healthcare challenges, creating accessible and effective tools that can improve patient care and medical outcomes.

Currently, I'm working on developing a mobile application that will allow people to conveniently order their medications online. While this app is designed to be inclusive for all users, it's particularly focused on helping Non-Communicable Disease (NCD) patients who need quick access to their medications during emergencies.

I'm excited about the future of digital health, telemedicine, health informatics, and how emerging technologies like AI and machine learning can revolutionize how we approach healthcare delivery and patient care.
